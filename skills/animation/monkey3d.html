Gorilla Coding Concept | 3D Animation

The Concept of this Ad is "Even animal also can learn coding"

This is my first 3D project completed in Blender, and it took almost 3 months. I encountered numerous challenges while creating this monkey ad, spanning from planning and sketching to modeling, lighting, and rendering. Originally, my founder requested this ad in 2D, but being keenly interested in 3D animations, I proposed doing it in a fun way in 3D, expressing confidence that it would be a big hit. He agreed with a simple 'okay.' Since we lacked a dedicated 3D Animation team, I took on the responsibility as the sole person knowledgeable about 3D. The idea was straightforward, but the animation was not. I recognized the complexity and challenges involved. At that time, I was not an expert in 3D, but the process of creating this ad served as a valuable learning experience, and I gained a significant amount of knowledge.
