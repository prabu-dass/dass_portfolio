Slate With Butterfly - 3D Animation

90s kids know that a slate is one of the most unforgettable tools during school studies, and I was inspired by that. So, I designed an SVG in Illustrator and then used Blender to design the slate. Finally, I added butterfly animation to bring positive vibes to the animation.

Software Used

Blender 3D | Premiere Pro | Illustrator | Photoshop
